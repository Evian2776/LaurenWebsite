Poetry



Beaten barren fields


Beaten barren fields

Meadows a white goods wasteland

Crows stand sentinel atop telephone wires

Fly cawing over the dumping ground

Fields of cars by the fishing lakes

Litter trails down the garden path

Fields torn up to build houses with nice views

All huddled together as if afraid of where they stand

Bare golf courses where weeds are weeds

Sheep grazing a ragged field

All is green

All will be lost
